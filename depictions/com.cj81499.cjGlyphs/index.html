If you want to use the included icons for something, contact me and ask permission.

Alternate icons have "ALT" after the @2x. When I make another style for an icon, it either replaces the default or becomes an ALT. I don't include new ALTs in the changelog. Remove the ALT after the @2x to use it instead of the default.

Info

This theme was made by tokems (Blog) and is being hosted by cj81499 (Contact).

The icon theme is from tokems' website, the control center is from hoenir's repo, and the theme was updated to work with iOS 8+ by cj81499.

A wallpaper for iPhone 5 or iPhone 6 is available at"Library/Themes/Glyphs - iOS 8.theme/Wallpaper".

Contains a Polus compatible CC theme.
